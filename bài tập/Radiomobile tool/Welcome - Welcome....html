Welcome...

To this website dedicated to Radio Mobile.

Radio Mobile is software by Roger Coudé VE2DBE. The program simulates RF propagation and is for free to the amateur radio community.

I hope that this website will be your guide for Radio Mobile and in to the exciting world of radio planning.

Cu, Remko (PE1MEW)
